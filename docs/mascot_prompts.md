# Prompts para la Generación de Gestos de Rabi

Para mantener la consistencia al usar herramientas de IA generativa de imágenes (como Midjourney, DALL-E, etc.), se debe usar un "Prompt Base" que define a la mascota, seguido de la "Acción/Gesto" específica para cada sección de la app.

## Prompt Base (Características Core)
*Copia y pega este texto al principio de cada instrucción:*

> **"A 2D vector illustration of a chibi-style anthropomorphic bipedal rabbit with a strictly stern and angry expression. The rabbit has a white base coat but features a tricolor calico/camo pattern with brown and dark gray patches on its body. It has large dark gray patches completely surrounding both eyes like a bandit mask. The inside of its long ears is dark gray; one ear is straight up, the other is folded. It has a tiny pink nose, bright pink circular blush spots on its cheeks, and a deeply furrowed brow. It wears a solid orange terracotta bandana tied around its neck. Thick dark black outlines, cel-shaded coloring, clean background."**

---

## Variaciones de Gestos y Acciones

Añade estas descripciones al final del "Prompt Base" para generar las diferentes posturas:

### 1. Pantalla de Inicio / Onboarding (El Reclutamiento)
*   **Contexto:** Evaluando al nuevo usuario.
*   **Añadir al prompt:** `The rabbit is standing in a military at-ease position, hands behind its back, feet slightly apart, leaning slightly forward, squinting its eyes to inspect and judge the viewer dismissively.`

### 2. Hábito Completado (La Aprobación Rígida)
*   **Contexto:** El usuario completó su tarea del día.
*   **Añadir al prompt:** `The rabbit is performing a firm and rigid military hand salute to its forehead. Its expression remains very serious and frowning, with no smile, showing strict military respect.`

### 3. Racha Perdida / Hábito Fallado (La Furia Desatada)
*   **Contexto:** El usuario falló un hábito o perdió la racha.
*   **Añadir al prompt:** `The rabbit is absolutely furious and screaming. Its mouth is wide open yelling aggressively, face flushed with anger. One hand is on its hip and the other hand is pointing its index finger directly and accusingly at the viewer. The orange bandana is blowing in the wind from the force of the yelling.`

### 4. Recordatorios / Notificaciones (Intimidación Táctica)
*   **Contexto:** Queda poco tiempo en el día para completar las tareas.
*   **Añadir al prompt:** `The rabbit is impatiently tapping a military command baton against the palm of its other hand, looking very impatient and giving a warning glare to the viewer. Wait and hurry up concept.`

### 5. Hitos y Rachas Largas (Respeto de Rango)
*   **Contexto:** El usuario ha logrado una meta de varios días seguidos.
*   **Añadir al prompt:** `The rabbit is standing proudly with its arms crossed over its chest. It has a very subtle, almost imperceptible smirk or half-smile, showing a rare moment of begrudging respect. It is holding a shiny military medal of honor hanging from its fingers.`

### 6. Pantalla Vacía (Vergüenza Ajena)
*   **Contexto:** El usuario no ha agregado ningún hábito.
*   **Añadir al prompt:** `The rabbit is doing a facepalm gesture, slapping one paw over its face and shaking its head in profound disappointment and secondhand embarrassment. Exhausted by the viewer's laziness.`
